Why Stand

Most apps teach kids about money. Stand lets them make it.

They learn by doing, not by listening.

No worksheets. No lectures. They set their own prices, write their own pitch, handle their own customers. The work is the lesson — and the lessons never stop.

Confidence that builds over time.

Day one they're proud of their logo. Week four they're pitching to strangers. Month three they're handling a bad week and figuring out what to change. That growth only happens with time.

Money makes more sense when it's theirs.

Pricing, profit margins, cost of materials, saving vs. spending. These concepts actually land when your kid earned the money themselves — and Stand teaches all of it.

We don't skip the hard weeks.

Not every idea works. Not every sale comes easy. Stand is built for those moments too — with tasks and lessons that help them push through instead of giving up.

For Parents

You don't have to teach any of it. That's our job.

Stand does the coaching. Your kid does the work. You get to watch.
