Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 20, 2026

Stand ("we," "us," "our") operates the website standkids.com and the web application at app.standkids.com (together, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it — especially when it comes to children.

Stand is designed for kids ages 8–14. We take that seriously. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and do not collect personal information from children without verified parental consent.

1. What We Collect

From Kids (Ages 8–14)

Before parental consent: Everything a child creates in Stand is stored only on their device (browser localStorage). We do not send any child data to our servers until a parent or guardian provides consent and completes a purchase. This means a child can use the full onboarding experience — naming a business, designing a brand, setting prices — without us ever seeing their data.

After parental consent: Once a parent consents and purchases a subscription, the following data is synced to our servers:

  • Child's first name (no last name is ever collected)
  • Age
  • Business information: business name, category, tagline, mission statement, brand colors, font preferences, product names, pricing
  • AI-generated content: logos, brand images, ad copy, pitch materials
  • Dashboard activity: task completion status, lesson progress

We do not collect a child's last name, email address, phone number, physical address, photo, or precise location.

From Parents/Guardians

  • Consent form: Name, email address, IP address (for audit purposes), and timestamp of consent
  • Checkout: Email address, shipping address (collected by Stripe, our payment processor)
  • Marketing site signup: Name, email, child's first name, child's age, and a profile description of the child's interests
  • SMS opt-in (optional): Mobile phone number, if you choose to opt in to text-message updates. Phone numbers are only stored if you check the SMS opt-in box during signup — they are never collected by default.

Automatically Collected

  • Cookies: We use minimal, functional cookies for session management through our database provider (Supabase). We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics.
  • Local storage: We store onboarding progress in your browser's localStorage so your child's work is saved between sessions.

3. AI and Third-Party Services

Stand uses AI to generate personalized business content for your child. Here's exactly what that means:

Anthropic (Claude AI)

We send your child's business details (business name, category, tagline, mission, and creative preferences) to Anthropic's Claude API to generate text content like taglines, mission statements, pitch materials, and lesson content. We do not send the child's name, age, or any parent data to the AI. Anthropic does not use API inputs for model training. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

Recraft (Image Generation)

We send business concept details (name, colors, style preferences) to Recraft's API to generate logos and brand images. No personal information is included in these requests. See Recraft's Privacy Policy.

Stripe (Payments)

Parent payment information (credit card, billing and shipping address) is collected and processed entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your full card number. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Resend (Email)

We use Resend to send transactional emails (parent join links, order confirmations). Parent email addresses are shared with Resend for delivery purposes only. See Resend's Privacy Policy.

ElevenLabs (Voice)

Stand offers optional voice-based lessons powered by ElevenLabs. No personal data is sent to ElevenLabs — only a secure session token is generated server-side. See ElevenLabs' Privacy Policy.

Supabase (Database & Hosting)

Our database is hosted on Supabase. All data described in this policy is stored in Supabase's infrastructure. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.

Vercel (Web Hosting)

Our website and application are hosted on Vercel. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.

Klaviyo (Email & SMS Marketing)

We use Klaviyo to send marketing emails and, where you have opted in, marketing text messages. Klaviyo receives the email address and (if you opt in) phone number you provide at signup, along with any engagement data (opens, clicks). We do not share child data with Klaviyo. See Klaviyo's Privacy Policy.

Stripe Connect (Storefront Payouts)

For families who choose to publish their child's storefront and accept real orders, the parent or guardian creates a Stripe Connected Account (Express). Stripe collects identity verification information (name, date of birth, last four of SSN, bank account) directly from the parent. Stand never sees or stores this information — it is exchanged between the parent and Stripe. See Stripe's Connected Account Agreement.

Printful / Printify (Fulfillment)

When a customer places an order through a kid's storefront, we share the shipping name, address, and product details with our print-on-demand fulfillment provider so they can print and ship the order. No child personal information is included in fulfillment requests.

4. SMS Marketing Program

If you check the SMS opt-in box at signup, Stand may send you recurring automated marketing text messages — examples: launch announcements, kit availability, your child's storefront milestones, and occasional promotional offers. You will receive no more than 6 messages per month.

  • Consent is optional. Opting in to SMS is never a condition of using Stand. The box is unchecked by default.
  • Msg & data rates may apply. Your wireless carrier's standard message and data rates apply to any messages we send.
  • Frequency: Up to 6 messages per month.
  • Opt out at any time: Reply STOP to any message to opt out. We'll send one confirmation and stop sending you marketing texts.
  • Help: Reply HELP or email support@standkids.com for support.
  • What we store: Phone number, opt-in timestamp, opt-in source URL, and engagement data (delivered, clicked, replied STOP).
  • We do not sell or share phone numbers with third-party advertisers. Phone numbers are shared only with Klaviyo (our SMS provider) and the carriers needed to deliver the message.
  • No sharing of opt-in or consent status with third parties. We do not share your SMS opt-in or consent status with any third party for purposes unrelated to providing you with this messaging service. Service providers (Klaviyo, our SMS-aggregator, and the wireless carriers needed to deliver messages) receive this information only to operate the program on our behalf, and may not use it for their own marketing or resale.

Stand does not send marketing text messages to anyone under 18. Phone numbers are only collected from parents and guardians.

5. Public Storefronts

Stand allows families to publish a public storefront page at standkids.com/store/[slug]. When a storefront is published:

  • The business name, the kid's first name only, the kid's age, the mission statement, and any AI-generated brand imagery (logos, product mockups, brand ads) become publicly visible and may be indexed by search engines.
  • Last names, email addresses, phone numbers, shipping addresses, and any other personally identifying information are never shown on a public storefront.
  • Parents may unpublish or modify the storefront at any time by emailing support@standkids.com.
  • Storefronts that include real-money checkout require the parent to complete Stripe Connect onboarding (see above).

6. Kid Sign-In and Authentication Cookies

When a kid signs in to Stand at standkids.com/sign-in, we use a small, secure HTTP cookie to remember that the device has been verified by a parent. This cookie:

  • Stores only a random session token (no name, no email, no kid PII).
  • Is HttpOnly and Secure — never readable by JavaScript or other websites.
  • Lasts up to 90 days, with sliding renewal on each visit.
  • Is used solely for authentication — that is, to keep the kid signed in across visits on the same device.
  • Is never used for marketing, advertising, retargeting, behavioral analytics, or any re-engagement purpose. Analytics cookies (if any) live in separate cookies with their own consent posture.

This use of a persistent identifier is permitted under COPPA's "support for the internal operations" exception (16 CFR §312.5(c)(7)), which allows persistent identifiers without separate verifiable parental consent when used to authenticate the user and maintain the integrity of the service.

The kid's 4-digit PIN is stored as a salted HMAC-SHA-256 hash with a server-side pepper — we cannot read the PIN. After 5 incorrect tries the profile is locked for 15 minutes, and a parent's involvement (via a fresh email code) is required to reset it.

7. COPPA Compliance

Stand is designed for children ages 8–14. Under COPPA, we are required to obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting personal information from children under 13. Here's how we do it:

  1. Local-first: Children can use Stand's onboarding without any data leaving their device. All progress is stored in browser localStorage.
  2. Parent gate: Before any child data is sent to our servers, a parent must visit a separate parent page, provide their name and email, check a consent box, and complete a purchase through Stripe.
  3. Audit trail: We record the parent's name, email, IP address, and timestamp of consent for compliance purposes.
  4. Minimal collection: We collect only a child's first name and age — never last names, emails, phone numbers, or photos.

8. Data Retention

We retain child and parent data for as long as the subscription is active. After a subscription ends:

  • Business data (logos, brand assets, business plans) is retained so the child keeps what they built.
  • Account data is retained for up to 12 months after the subscription ends, then deleted unless the parent requests earlier deletion.
  • Consent records are retained for 3 years for legal compliance.
  • Payment records are retained as required by law and Stripe's policies.

9. Data Deletion

Parents can request deletion of their child's data at any time by emailing support@standkids.com. Upon receiving a verified request, we will:

  • Delete all child data from our servers within 30 days
  • Request deletion from third-party sub-processors where applicable
  • Confirm deletion by email

Note: Data stored only in the child's browser (localStorage) is not on our servers and can be cleared by the user at any time.

10. Data Security

We protect your data with:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit
  • Server-side API keys — secrets are never exposed to the browser
  • Role-based database access — the browser client has limited permissions; sensitive operations use a server-side service key
  • Content moderation — a profanity and safety filter screens all child-entered text

No system is 100% secure. If we discover a breach that affects personal information, we will notify affected parents by email within 72 hours.

11. Your Rights

Parents and guardians have the right to:

  • Review the personal information we've collected from their child
  • Delete their child's personal information
  • Refuse further collection (by canceling the subscription)
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email support@standkids.com.

12. California Residents

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what data we collect, request deletion, and opt out of data sales. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your CCPA rights, email support@standkids.com.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes — especially to how we handle children's data — we will notify parents by email and post the updated policy here with a new effective date.
